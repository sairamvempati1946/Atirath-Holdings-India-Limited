Vendor Requirements

Basic eligibility criteria

Documentation

  • GST Registration Certificate
  • PAN Card of the entity
  • MSME / Udyam Registration (if applicable)
  • Previous work experience/credentials
  • Bank account details & cancelled cheque
  • ISO or other quality certifications

Eligibility

  • Registered business entity in India
  • Minimum 2 years of relevant experience
  • Financial stability and turnover criteria
  • Quality assurance systems in place
  • Commitment to timely delivery
  • Compliance with statutory regulations

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about vendor partnership
How long does vendor registration take?
The registration and verification process typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on the completeness of documents submitted.
Is there any registration fee?
No, vendor registration with Atirath Holdings is completely free. There are no hidden charges.
What is the payment terms?
Standard payment terms are 30-45 days from invoice date, subject to quality acceptance.
Can small businesses apply?
Yes, we encourage MSMEs and startups to apply. We have special provisions for small businesses meeting quality standards.
How are vendors selected?
Vendors are selected based on technical capability, financial stability, quality standards, and competitive pricing.
Is there an annual renewal process?
Yes, vendor empanelment is reviewed annually based on performance and compliance.
