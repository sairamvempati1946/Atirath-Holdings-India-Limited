Our Company

Atirath Holdings India Limited is a forward-thinking energy company committed to building India's clean fuel infrastructure through innovative bio-gas solutions. We are a leading EPC company specializing in setting up CBG (Compressed Biogas) manufacturing plants across India. With cutting-edge technology and comprehensive assistance, we help entrepreneurs establish their own CBG manufacturing plants, contributing to India's green energy revolution.

At Atirath Holdings, we understand the urgent need for sustainable and renewable energy sources. CBG, also known as Bio-CNG, is produced from organic waste such as agricultural residues, Napier Grass, food waste, and animal manure through biomethanation. It's a clean, renewable, and carbon-neutral fuel for transportation, cooking, and electricity generation.

Our team of experienced professionals provides end-to-end solutions - from project conceptualization and feasibility studies to design, engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning. We utilize state-of-the-art technology and adhere to international standards, ensuring high-quality and efficient CBG production.

We invite entrepreneurs across India to partner with us in setting up CBG manufacturing plants. As a partner, you benefit from our expertise, technical know-how, industry insights, and strong network of suppliers and regulatory authorities. We provide customized solutions tailored to your requirements and ensure compliance with all regulations.

Join us in creating a greener and more sustainable future for India. Contact us today to explore how we can assist you in setting up a CBG manufacturing plant at your location!
