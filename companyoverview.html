Atirath Holdings India Limited

is a forward-thinking energy company dedicated to transforming India's energy landscape through sustainable bio-gas solutions. Founded with a vision to create a greener planet, we specialize in setting up state-of-the-art Compressed Biogas (CBG) manufacturing plants across the nation.

As a leading EPC company, we provide end-to-end solutions for CBG plant setup - from project conceptualization and feasibility studies to design, engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning. We utilize cutting-edge technology and adhere to international standards, ensuring high-quality and efficient CBG production.

Our mission is to produce high-quality CBG from renewable biomass sources like Napier Grass, agricultural residues, and organic waste. We are committed to creating rural livelihoods, reducing carbon emissions, and contributing to India's net-zero goals.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and innovative clean energy company, leading the transition towards a sustainable, carbon-neutral future by making Compressed Biogas accessible, affordable, and abundant for all.

Our Mission

To transform organic waste into valuable energy resources through cutting-edge technology, create sustainable livelihoods in rural communities, reduce India's dependency on fossil fuels, and contribute to a cleaner, greener planet for future generations.

Our Journey

2018
Company Founded
Atirath Holdings India Limited was established with a vision to revolutionize India's clean energy sector through CBG technology.
2019
First CBG Plant Project
Commenced our first CBG manufacturing plant project in Telangana, marking our entry into renewable energy production.
2020
SATAT Scheme Partnership
Became an official partner under Government of India's SATAT scheme for CBG plant setup across the nation.
2021
Napier Grass Cultivation
Launched large-scale Napier Grass cultivation program across 50+ acres, empowering local farmers.
2022
Expansion Across India
Expanded operations to multiple states including Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Karnataka.
2023
500+ TPD Capacity
Achieved cumulative CBG production capacity of over 500 TPD across our operational plants.
2024
100+ Rural Jobs Created
Created sustainable employment opportunities for over 100 rural households through our operations.
