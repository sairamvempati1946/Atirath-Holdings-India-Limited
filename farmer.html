Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our farmer partnership program
What is the minimum land required?
Farmers can participate with as little as 1 acre of land. However, larger areas (5+ acres) are preferred for better economies of scale.
Do you provide the planting material?
Yes, we provide high-quality Napier Grass cuttings/slips at subsidized rates to our partner farmers.
How is the buyback price determined?
Buyback prices are fixed in the agreement and revised annually based on market conditions, ensuring fair returns.
Do you provide transportation?
Yes, we arrange doorstep collection of harvested Napier Grass from your farm at no extra cost.
Is there any subsidy available?
Yes, we assist farmers in availing government subsidies for green energy crops and agricultural equipment.
What is the contract duration?
The partnership agreement is typically for 5 years, renewable based on mutual consent.
