Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions
How long does it take to set up a CBG plant?
The typical timeline for a CBG plant setup is 12-18 months from land acquisition to commissioning, depending on plant capacity and regulatory approvals.
What is the minimum investment required?
The investment ranges from ₹10 crore to ₹100 crore+ based on plant capacity, technology selection, and location. We provide detailed project reports for each case.
Do you provide technical support after plant setup?
Yes, we offer comprehensive after-sales support including training, maintenance contracts, and operational guidance for the lifetime of the plant.
How do I partner with Atirath Holdings?
You can fill out the inquiry form on this page or directly contact our partnership team. We offer collaboration models for investors, landowners, and entrepreneurs.
What government subsidies are available?
Government provides capital subsidies up to 30%, income tax holiday for 5 years, GST benefits, and collateral-free loans under the SATAT scheme.
Is there a buyback guarantee for CBG production?
Yes, we have partnerships with OMCs for guaranteed offtake of CBG produced at our plants, ensuring revenue certainty.
