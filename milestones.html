Our Journey So Far

Tracking our progress from inception to becoming a leading CBG manufacturer in India
2018
Company Founded
Atirath Holdings India Limited was established with a vision to revolutionize India's clean energy sector through CBG technology. Started with a small team of passionate renewable energy experts.
2019
First CBG Plant Project
Commenced our first CBG manufacturing plant project in Telangana, marking our entry into renewable energy production. Successfully commissioned 50 TPD capacity plant.
2020
SATAT Scheme Partnership
Became an official partner under Government of India's SATAT (Sustainable Alternative Towards Affordable Transportation) scheme for CBG plant setup across the nation.
2021
Napier Grass Cultivation Launch
Launched large-scale Napier Grass cultivation program across 50+ acres, empowering local farmers with sustainable income and ensuring consistent feedstock supply.
2022
Expansion Across India
Expanded operations to multiple states including Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Karnataka. Established 3 new CBG plants with advanced purification technology.
2023
500+ TPD Capacity Milestone
Achieved cumulative CBG production capacity of over 500 TPD across our operational plants. Recognized as one of India's fastest-growing clean energy companies.
2024
100+ Rural Jobs Created
Created sustainable employment opportunities for over 100 rural households through our operations and farmer partnerships. Established robust supply chain network.
